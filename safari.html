Safari

Canadian Wilds Adventure

Embark on a journey through the heart of Ontario's wilderness in the Canadian Wilds Adventure. From the majestic moose to the elusive Canadian lynx, this exhibit brings you face to face with the diverse wildlife that thrives in the province. Traverse the landscapes of boreal forests, wetlands, and open meadows as you learn about the intricate connections that sustain Ontario's natural beauty.

Ontario Wetlands Safari

Step into the vibrant world of wetlands at the Ontario Wetlands Safari. From vibrant waterfowl to turtles and frogs, this exhibit showcases the incredible biodiversity that relies on these critical ecosystems. As you navigate through this area, you'll discover the essential roles wetlands play in filtering water and providing habitats for countless species.

Boreal Forest Adventure

Enter the serene landscapes of Ontario's boreal forest at the Boreal Forest Adventure. Encounter wolves, caribou, and other northern species as you explore this captivating exhibit. Through informative displays and engaging animal behaviors, you'll gain insights into the intricacies of this unique woodland ecosystem.

Cave Bat Adventure

Explore the mysterious world of Ontario's bats at the Cave Bat Adventure. Learn about the vital role these creatures play in controlling insect populations and maintaining ecosystem balance. Through informative displays and simulated cave environments, you'll gain a deeper appreciation for the often-misunderstood residents of the night sky.
