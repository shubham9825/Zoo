About Us

Welcome to the Toronto Zoo

A captivating world where the wonders of nature come alive! As one of the largest and most renowned zoos in the world, we're dedicated to creating an unforgettable experience that brings you face-to-face with the incredible diversity of life on Earth.

Nestled in the heart of Toronto, our expansive grounds are home to over 5,000 animals representing more than 450 species. From the lush tropics to the frozen tundra, each meticulously designed habitat offers a glimpse into the captivating lives of animals from around the globe. Whether you're captivated by the playful antics of our giant pandas, mesmerized by the grace of our big cats, or intrigued by the underwater worlds of our aquatic residents, there's a world of discovery waiting for you at every turn.

Who we are

Our history

Since its inception, the history of the Toronto Zoo has been a tale of unwavering dedication to wildlife conservation, education, and unforgettable experiences. Established in 1974, the zoo has evolved from a modest collection of animals into a global leader in zoological excellence.

The Toronto Zoo's story is one of growth and innovation. Situated on sprawling grounds that mirror diverse ecosystems from around the world, the zoo has continuously expanded its exhibits and habitats to provide animals with environments that closely resemble their natural homes. From the majestic African Savanna to the immersive Tundra Trek, each exhibit is a testament to our commitment to animal welfare and educational engagement.

Our history is deeply intertwined with milestones of conservation. Through strategic partnerships with international organizations and collaborations with experts in the field, the Toronto Zoo has played a pivotal role in numerous critical initiatives to protect endangered species and ecosystems.

Inspiring a Future for Wildlife

At the Toronto Zoo, we are more than just a destination - we are a sanctuary of discovery, education, and conservation. Our unwavering commitment to wildlife and the environment has shaped us into a globally acclaimed institution, recognized for our dedication to preserving Earth's astonishing biodiversity.

A Living Tapestry of Nature

As one of the world's most prominent zoological establishments, the Toronto Zoo boasts a sprawling landscape meticulously designed to mirror ecosystems from across the globe. With over 5,000 animals representing more than 450 species, we have created a living tapestry that narrates the stories of countless wildlife wonders.

Guardians of Tomorrow's Wilderness

Beyond our exhibits, the Toronto Zoo is a beacon of hope for endangered species and threatened habitats. Collaborating with international partners, we are at the forefront of critical conservation initiatives, aiming not just to save species, but to inspire a generation of conservationists. By fostering a deep connection between people and the natural world, we are shaping a future where humans and wildlife coexist harmoniously.
